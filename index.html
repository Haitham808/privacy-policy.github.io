Privacy Policy

Application: Kids Study Timer

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

1. Commitment to Kids' Privacy

The Kids Study Timer is designed specifically for children and families. We strictly follow the Apple Made for Kids guidelines. This application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information from children.

2. Purchases & Data Minimization

We use RevenueCat to manage lifetime premium access. RevenueCat only processes an anonymous App User ID to verify your purchase. This data is not linked to your identity. We do not collect:

  • Names, Emails, or Phone Numbers
  • Contacts or Location Data
  • Tracking identifiers for advertising

3. Parental Gate

To comply with COPPA and global privacy standards, any external-facing features or in-app purchase triggers are protected by a Parental Gate. This ensures that only adults can interact with system-level features or financial transactions.

4. Local Data Only

All app progress, character customizations, and timer settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your data. If the app is deleted, this local data is removed according to standard iOS procedures.

Support & Feedback

For technical issues or billing inquiries related to in-app purchases, please use the standard Apple App Store Support channels associated with your Apple Account.
