Privacy Policy

Application: CDL Pre-Trip Inspection 2026

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Data Collection & Privacy

The CDL Pre-Trip Inspection 2026 app is designed with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We believe your professional data belongs to you. To ensure maximum privacy and offline reliability, this application **does not collect, transmit, or share** any personal information, location data, or device identifiers with external servers.

2. Local-Only Storage

All inspection data, checklists, and defect logs are stored exclusively on your local device using secure internal storage. This data is not accessible by the developer or any third-party entities.

3. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted over the internet, your information is protected from remote interception. We recommend using standard device security features (passcode, biometric lock) to protect the data stored on your phone.
