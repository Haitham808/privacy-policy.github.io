Privacy Policy

Application: Kids Study Timer

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

1. Commitment to Kids' Privacy

The Kids Study Timer is designed specifically for children. We strictly follow the Apple Made for Kids guidelines. We do not collect personal information from children, nor do we include any third-party advertising or social media integration.

2. Purchases & Data Minimization

We use RevenueCat to manage lifetime premium access. RevenueCat only processes an anonymous App User ID to verify your purchase. We do not collect:

  • Names or Email Addresses
  • Phone Numbers or Contacts
  • Precise Location Data
  • Device identifiers that track you across other apps

3. Parental Controls

To comply with COPPA and Apple's policies, all external links (such as support emails) and in-app purchases are protected by a Parental Gate. This ensures that only adults can access settings or make financial transactions.

4. Local Storage

Your child's room progress, character unlocks, and timer settings are stored locally on your device. This data is never sent to our servers and is deleted if the app is uninstalled.
