1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use Timer for Kids – Routines (the “App”).

2) Information we collect

Routines and tasks are stored on your device only. We do not upload your routine content to our servers.

  • On-device content: routines, tasks, timers, and preferences you create in the App are saved locally on your device.
  • Purchase information: if you make an in-app purchase, Apple processes the payment. We receive purchase entitlement status (e.g., “premium unlocked”) to enable features.

We do not collect analytics and we do not collect advertising identifiers.

3) Children’s privacy

The App is designed for families and may be used by children with parental guidance. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  • We do not require a child to provide a name, email address, phone number, or location to use the App.
  • If you believe we may have collected personal information from a child, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

4) How we use information

  • To provide core features (timers, routines, reminders, and progress display).
  • To unlock premium access after a purchase is verified.

5) In-app purchases (RevenueCat)

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details (such as credit card numbers).

We use RevenueCat to help manage purchase entitlements and restore access across devices. RevenueCat may process limited transaction-related data such as:

  • transaction identifiers
  • purchase timestamps
  • entitlement status (e.g., premium unlocked)
  • country/region pricing metadata

Routine content (tasks, notes, timers) is not sent to RevenueCat.

6) Advertising

We do not show third-party advertising in the App.

7) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only when necessary:

  • Service providers: RevenueCat for purchase entitlement management.
  • Legal reasons: if required to comply with law or protect rights, safety, and security.

8) Data storage & deletion

  • On-device storage: routines and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Delete your data: you can delete app data by uninstalling the App (or using any in-app delete options if provided).

9) Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.
